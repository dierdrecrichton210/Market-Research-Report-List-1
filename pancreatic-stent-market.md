<p><p>The pancreatic stent market is divided into two main types: plastic pancreatic stents and metal pancreatic stents. Plastic pancreatic stents are typically flexible, cost-effective, and commonly used for temporary drainage, making them suitable for short-term applications. In contrast, metal pancreatic stents are more durable, offering longer-term solutions for patients requiring extended drainage due to conditions like chronic pancreatitis or pancreatic obstructions. Both types aim to alleviate symptoms and improve quality of life in patients with pancreatic disorders.</p></p>
