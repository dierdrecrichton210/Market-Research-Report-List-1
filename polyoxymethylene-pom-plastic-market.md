<p><p>The Polyoxymethylene (POM) plastic market is primarily categorized into two types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene is characterized by its high crystalline structure, offering superior strength and rigidity, making it ideal for precision engineering applications. In contrast, co-polyoxymethylene features a modified polymer composition that provides enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, catering to a wider range of applications. Both types are utilized in industries like automotive, consumer goods, and electronics due to their excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance.</p></p>
