<p><p>The etching metal product market is primarily categorized into two types: wet etching and dry etching. Wet etching utilizes liquid chemicals to remove material from metal surfaces, offering high uniformity and precision suitable for intricate designs. Conversely, dry etching employs gases and plasma to achieve etching, allowing for better control over etch depth and sidewall profiles, making it ideal for advanced applications. Both methods play crucial roles in industries such as electronics, manufacturing, and microfabrication, catering to diverse customer needs.</p></p>
