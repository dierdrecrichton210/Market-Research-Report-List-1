<p><p>Tire retreading for commercial vehicles involves renewing worn tires by replacing the tread, extending their lifespan and reducing costs. The market is divided into Pre Cure and Mold Cure methods. Pre Cure uses pre-molded tread strips bonded to the tire casing, offering flexibility and faster processing. Mold Cure involves applying raw rubber to the casing and curing it in a mold, providing durability and a new-tire-like finish. Both methods cater to cost-effective, sustainable tire solutions for commercial fleets.</p></p>
