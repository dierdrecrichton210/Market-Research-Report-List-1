<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) involves tracking glucose levels in real-time, enabling better diabetes management. The market comprises three main types: transmitters, which send glucose data from sensors to a display device; sensors, which measure glucose levels in interstitial fluid; and insulin pumps, which deliver insulin based on CGM readings. These components work together to provide users with critical information, allowing for timely adjustments in insulin administration and lifestyle choices, ultimately improving overall health outcomes for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
