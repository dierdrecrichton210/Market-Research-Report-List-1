<p><strong>The Automotive Seal Component Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The automotive seal component market includes materials like EPDM, NR, and SBR, each offering unique properties. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is widely used for its excellent weather, ozone, and temperature resistance, making it ideal for door seals and window channels. NR (Natural Rubber) provides superior elasticity and flexibility, often used in dynamic seals. SBR (Styrene Butadiene Rubber) offers good abrasion resistance and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in gaskets and vibration dampers. These materials cater to diverse automotive sealing needs.</p></p>
