<p><p>The brake friction products market includes various components essential for vehicle braking systems. Brake pads, typically found in disc brakes, provide necessary friction to slow or stop vehicles. Brake shoes work with drum brakes, creating friction against the drum surface. Brake liners, used in some applications, provide a replaceable friction surface for braking. Additionally, drum and disc products represent the two primary brake systems, with drums encasing brake shoes and discs paired with brake pads. Each type plays a crucial role in vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
