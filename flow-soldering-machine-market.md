<p><strong>The Flow Soldering Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Flow Soldering Machine</li><li>Vapour Phase Flow Soldering Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow soldering machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Convection Flow Soldering Machines and Vapour Phase Flow Soldering Machines. Convection flow soldering utilizes heated airflow to melt solder, ensuring even temperature distribution, ideal for complex boards. In contrast, vapour phase flow soldering employs vaporized solvents to create a consistent thermal environment, allowing for precise soldering even on delicate components. Both technologies cater to different production needs, offering distinct advantages in efficiency, precision, and thermal control.</p></p>
