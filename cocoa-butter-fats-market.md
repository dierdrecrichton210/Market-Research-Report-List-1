<p><p>The cocoa butter fats market encompasses various types, including cocoa butter equivalents (CBEs), cocoa butter substitutes (CBSs), and others. CBEs are vegetable fats designed to mimic cocoa butter's properties, used in chocolate and confectionery to enhance texture and stability. CBSs, on the other hand, offer a cost-effective alternative while maintaining similar melting and flavor characteristics. The "others" category includes a range of specialized fats derived from different sources, catering to diverse applications in food formulations and industrial uses.</p></p>
