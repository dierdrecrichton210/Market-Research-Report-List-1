<p><ul><li>Induction Tachogenerators</li><li>Synchronous Tachogenerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC tachogenerators are vital for measuring rotational speed in various industrial applications. The market includes two primary types: induction tachogenerators and synchronous tachogenerators. Induction tachogenerators operate on the principle of electromagnetic induction, providing a voltage output proportional to the rotational speed. In contrast, synchronous tachogenerators maintain a constant speed, producing a steady output regardless of variations in load. Both types serve critical roles in automation, robotics, and control systems, contributing to precise speed monitoring and enhanced operational efficiency.</p></p>
