<p><p>The skier helmets market is categorized primarily into two types based on material: ABS and PC. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) helmets are known for their robust durability and impact resistance, offering good protection at an affordable price. In contrast, PC (Polycarbonate) helmets are lighter and provide enhanced strength and flexibility, making them comfortable for extended wear while maintaining high safety standards. Each type caters to different preferences in terms of performance, comfort, and cost within the skiing community.</p></p>
