<p><p>The urban rail signaling systems market encompasses various operational types, including Semi-Automatic Train Operation (SATO), Driverless Train Operation (DTO), and Unattended Train Operation (UTO). SATO combines human intervention with automated systems, enhancing operational efficiency and safety. DTO eliminates the need for onboard drivers, relying entirely on automated control, while UTO takes this further by requiring minimal human oversight, optimizing capacity and reducing costs. Each type aims to improve urban transit efficiency, safety, and passenger experience through advanced technology.</p></p>
