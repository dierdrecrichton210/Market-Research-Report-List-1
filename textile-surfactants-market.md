<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Textile Surfactants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic</li><li>Cationic</li><li>Non-ionic</li><li>Amphoteric/Zwitterionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile surfactants market is segmented into four main types: anionic, cationic, non-ionic, and amphoteric/zwitterionic surfactants. Anionic surfactants are negatively charged and effective in cleaning and foaming. Cationic surfactants carry a positive charge, offering antimicrobial properties and fabric softening. Non-ionic surfactants, which have no charge, provide excellent wetting and emulsifying capabilities. Amphoteric or zwitterionic surfactants can carry both positive and negative charges, making them versatile for various applications in textile processing and finishing.</p></p>
