<p><p>The PET-CT scanning services market comprises two primary technologies: Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and X-Ray Computed Tomography (CT) scanners. PET scanners visualize metabolic processes by detecting radioactive tracers, enabling the identification of diseases like cancer. CT scanners provide detailed cross-sectional images of tissues and organs using X-rays, essential for precise anatomical insights. The integration of PET and CT enhances diagnostic accuracy, facilitating better treatment planning and patient management, driving demand for these advanced imaging techniques in healthcare settings.</p></p>
