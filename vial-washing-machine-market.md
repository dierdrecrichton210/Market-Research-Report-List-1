<p><p>The vial washing machine market features two primary types: linear tunnel and rotary. Linear tunnel machines provide a continuous washing process, where vials move through a series of washing stations in a straight line, ensuring efficient cleaning of large volumes. In contrast, rotary machines employ a revolving mechanism, allowing vials to be washed in batches, which is ideal for smaller operations or varying vial sizes. Both types cater to pharmaceutical and laboratory needs, focusing on cleanliness and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
