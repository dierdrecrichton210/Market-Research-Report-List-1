<p><p>Exterior Insulation and Finish System (EIFS) is a building exterior wall system that provides insulation, moisture management, and a decorative finish. The market is primarily segmented into two types: polymer-based and polymer-modified EIFS. Polymer-based EIFS utilizes a continuous layer of insulation covered by a thin synthetic finish, offering high flexibility and moisture resistance. Polymer-modified EIFS, on the other hand, incorporates additives to enhance durability, adhesion, and performance, making it suitable for various climates and enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
