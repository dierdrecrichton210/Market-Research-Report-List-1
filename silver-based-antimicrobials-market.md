<p><p>The silver-based antimicrobials market features various carrier types that enhance silver's efficacy in combating microbes. Silicate carriers utilize silica matrices to improve silver stability and release. Phosphate carriers incorporate silver into phosphate compounds for sustained antibacterial action. Titanium dioxide carriers provide photocatalytic properties, enhancing antimicrobial effectiveness under UV light. Glass carriers, on the other hand, integrate silver into glass materials, offering durable and versatile applications in healthcare, textiles, and coatings while ensuring controlled silver release for long-lasting protection.</p></p>
