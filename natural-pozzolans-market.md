<p><p>The Natural Pozzolans Market is divided into two main types: Raw Pozzolan and Calcined Pozzolan. Raw Pozzolan consists of naturally occurring materials, such as volcanic ash, that enhance the properties of cement and concrete when mixed. Calcined Pozzolan is produced by heating raw pozzolans to high temperatures, which increases their reactivity and improves their bonding capabilities. Both types are valued for their sustainability, performance in construction applications, and contribution to reducing the carbon footprint of concrete production.</p></p>
