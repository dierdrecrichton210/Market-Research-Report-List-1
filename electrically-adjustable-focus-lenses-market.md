<p><p>The Electrically Adjustable Focus Lenses market primarily comprises two key types: electrowetting-based liquid lenses and liquid crystal lenses. Electrowetting-based lenses utilize the principle of electrowetting to adjust the curvature of a liquid interface, enabling rapid focus changes and offering a compact design for applications such as cameras and smartphones. Liquid crystal lenses, on the other hand, leverage the refractive properties of liquid crystals, allowing for electronic control of focus through voltage application, suitable for various optical and display devices.</p></p>
