<p><p>The Hemostatic Analyzer Market encompasses various types of tests vital for assessing blood coagulation. Routine Coagulation Tests include Prothrombin Time (PT) and International Normalized Ratio (INR), crucial for monitoring anticoagulant therapy. Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (APTT) evaluates intrinsic pathway factors, while Thrombin Time (TT) assesses fibrinogen function. Fibrinogen tests gauge clot formation potential, and D-Dimer tests help diagnose thrombotic disorders. Additionally, Diagnostic Hemostasis Assays, Platelet Function Testing, and Thrombophilia Testing further aid in evaluating bleeding disorders and thrombosis risks.</p></p>
