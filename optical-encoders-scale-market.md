<p><strong>The Optical Encoders Scale Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Scales</li><li>Partial Arc Scales</li><li>Rotary Scales</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoders scale market is divided into three primary types: linear scales, partial arc scales, and rotary scales. Linear scales are used for measuring linear displacements with high precision, often found in CNC machines and industrial automation. Partial arc scales measure rotational movement over a limited range, ideal for applications needing specific angular accuracy. Rotary scales, on the other hand, are designed for complete rotational movement, commonly employed in robotics and automation to track full 360-degree rotations.</p></p>
