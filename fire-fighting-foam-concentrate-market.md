<p><p>The firefighting foam concentrate market is primarily divided into two categories: Class A foam and Class B foam. Class A foam is designed for combating ordinary combustible materials like wood and paper, enhancing water's effectiveness by reducing surface tension and improving penetration. Conversely, Class B foam targets flammable liquids such as gasoline and oils, forming a blanket that suppresses vapor and prevents re-ignition. Both types are vital in different firefighting scenarios, ensuring comprehensive fire control and safety.</p></p>
