<p><p>The aircraft cooling turbine market is primarily divided into two types: gas cooling turbines and water cooling turbines. Gas cooling turbines utilize air or gas to absorb and dissipate heat generated by engine operations, enhancing efficiency and performance. In contrast, water cooling turbines employ water as a cooling medium, effectively managing thermal loads. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in aircraft engines, addressing the challenges of heat management, and ensuring reliability and safety in aviation operations.</p></p>
