<p><ul><li>Brewed</li><li>Blended</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy sauce market can be categorized into two main types: brewed and blended. Brewed soy sauce is made through a natural fermentation process, employing soybeans, wheat, and specific molds, leading to a rich flavor profile. In contrast, blended soy sauce combines brewed and chemically produced components, resulting in a more consistent taste and often lower production costs. The choice between these types often depends on consumer preferences for authenticity versus convenience and price, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
