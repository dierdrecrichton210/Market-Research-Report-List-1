<p><p>The aquatic herbicides market comprises various chemical types, including glyphosate, imazapyr, diquat, and triclopyr. Glyphosate is a broad-spectrum herbicide effective against many aquatic weeds. Imazapyr targets tough, perennial plants, offering residual control. Diquat is a fast-acting herbicide suitable for controlling floating and submerged vegetation. Triclopyr is particularly effective on woody and invasive species. Each herbicide serves distinct purposes based on its chemical properties, efficacy, and application methods in managing aquatic ecosystems and maintaining water quality.</p></p>
