<p><strong>The Automotive Active Window Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combiner Technology</li><li>Windshield Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive active window display market is categorized into two main types: combiner technology and windshield technology. Combiner technology projects information onto a transparent surface that overlays the driver’s view, allowing for augmented reality experiences without obstructing sight. In contrast, windshield technology integrates display components directly into the vehicle's windshield, projecting critical data onto the glass itself. Both technologies aim to enhance driver awareness and safety by providing real-time information while maintaining the aesthetics and functionality of the vehicle.</p></p>
