<p><p>The SURF (Subsea Umbilicals, Risers, and Flowlines) market encompasses three primary components essential for offshore oil and gas production. Umbilicals are cable systems that supply power, communication, and control fluids to subsea equipment. Risers are vertical pipes that connect the seabed infrastructure to surface facilities, facilitating the transport of oil and gas. Flowlines are pipelines that transport hydrocarbons from subsea wells to processing facilities. Together, these elements enable efficient and safe extraction of resources from beneath the ocean.</p></p>
