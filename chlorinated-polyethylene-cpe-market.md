<p><p>Chlorinated Polyethylene (CPE) is a versatile polymer used in various applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: Thermoplastic Resin Type and Elastomer Rubber Type. The Thermoplastic Resin Type offers high strength, heat resistance, and is suitable for applications such as coatings and cable insulation. In contrast, the Elastomer Rubber Type provides flexibility, impact resistance, and is ideal for sealing applications, gaskets, and other uses where durability and elasticity are essential. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
