<p><p>The solvent-free packaging adhesive market primarily consists of two types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that do not require mixing, providing convenience and ease of application. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and a hardener before use, offering superior bonding strength and versatility for various materials. Both types are environmentally friendly options that help reduce volatile organic compound emissions, making them suitable for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
