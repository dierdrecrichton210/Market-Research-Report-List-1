Segmented molds consist of multiple interchangeable segments, allowing flexibility in tire design and easier maintenance, ideal for producing complex tread patterns. Two-piece molds are simpler, comprising two halves that clamp together, suitable for standard tire designs and mass production. Both types cater to varying manufacturing needs, with segmented molds offering customization and two-piece molds emphasizing efficiency and cost-effectiveness in high-volume production.</p></p>
