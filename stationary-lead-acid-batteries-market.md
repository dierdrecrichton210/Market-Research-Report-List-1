<p><strong>The Stationary Lead Acid Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cell</li><li>Valve Regulated Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary lead-acid battery market is primarily divided into two types: open cell and valve-regulated lead-acid (VRLA) batteries. Open cell batteries, typically used in applications requiring high reliability and capacity, need regular maintenance and electrolyte level monitoring. In contrast, VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, offering better safety and convenience, making them suitable for backup power systems and renewable energy applications. Both types play crucial roles in energy storage, emergency systems, and telecommunications.</p></p>
