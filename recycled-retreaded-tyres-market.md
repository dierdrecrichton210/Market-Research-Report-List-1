<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled (retreaded) tyre market consists primarily of two types: pre-cure and mold cure. Pre-cure retreading involves applying a pre-vulcanized tread onto the tyre casing, allowing for flexibility in design and tread patterns. In contrast, mold cure retreading involves shaping new treads directly onto the tyre under heat and pressure in a mold, creating a uniform surface. Both methods aim to extend tyre lifespan while promoting sustainability by reusing materials and reducing waste in the tyre industry.</p></p>
