<p><p>The roach bait trap market comprises various types designed for effective pest control. Granular baits consist of small particles that can be spread in infested areas, appealing to roaches as they forage. Gel baits are viscous formulations that adhere to surfaces, targeting roaches directly and providing prolonged efficacy.
