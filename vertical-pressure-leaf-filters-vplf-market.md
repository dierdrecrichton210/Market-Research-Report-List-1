<p><p>Vertical Pressure Leaf Filters (VPLFs) are industrial filtration systems used primarily for separating solids from liquids in various processes. The market segments are categorized into dry cake discharge and wet cake discharge. Dry cake discharge VPLFs allow for efficient removal of the solid filter cake, facilitating easier handling, transport, and storage. In contrast, wet cake discharge VPLFs retain the moisture within the cake, often preferred for applications that require further processing of the solids. Both types cater to different operational needs and applications.</p></p>
