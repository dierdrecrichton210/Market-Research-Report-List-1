<p><p>The solar home battery market features three main types: Li-ion, Lead-Acid, and Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) batteries. Li-ion batteries are known for their high energy density, longer lifespan, and efficiency, making them popular for residential solar systems. Lead-Acid batteries, while more affordable, have a shorter lifespan and lower energy density. LFP batteries offer enhanced safety, longer cycle life, and thermal stability, positioning them as a growing choice for eco-conscious consumers seeking reliable energy storage solutions.</p></p>
