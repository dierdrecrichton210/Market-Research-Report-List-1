<p><p>A Continuous Plastic Pyrolysis Plant is an advanced facility designed to convert plastic waste into valuable resources through a pyrolysis process that operates continuously rather than in batch cycles. This technology efficiently decomposes plastics at high temperatures in an oxygen-deficient environment, yielding products such as fuel oil, carbon black, and gas. The demand for these plants is increasing due to the urgent need for sustainable waste management solutions and the growing awareness of environmental pollution caused by plastic waste.</p><p>The Continuous Plastic Pyrolysis Plant Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period.
