Dual IR detectors use two wavelengths to reduce false alarms and enhance accuracy, particularly in environments with potential interference. Multi IR detectors employ multiple wavelengths, offering even greater sensitivity and reliability, making them suitable for complex industrial settings where precise flame detection is critical.</p></p>
