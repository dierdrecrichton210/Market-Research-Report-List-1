<p><p>Thermal Cycling Thermoelectric Coolers (TECs) utilize the Peltier effect to create a temperature differential, enabling efficient cooling and heating. The market is predominantly segmented into two types: Single Stage Thermal Cycling TECs, which provide moderate cooling performance for applications with limited temperature differentials, and Multistage Thermal Cycling TECs, designed for applications requiring substantial temperature drops. Multistage TECs utilize multiple thermoelectric couples to achieve lower temperatures, making them suitable for advanced cooling solutions in various industries, including electronics and medical devices.</p></p>
