<p><p>The carbon fiber wind turbine blade market features two primary manufacturing techniques: Hand Lay-Up Molding and Resin Transfer Molding. Hand Lay-Up Molding involves manually placing layers of carbon fiber fabric and resin into a mold, offering flexibility but requiring significant labor. In contrast, Resin Transfer Molding automates the infusion of resin into pre-placed fiber, allowing for better consistency and efficiency. Both methods are crucial for producing lightweight, durable blades that enhance wind turbine performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
