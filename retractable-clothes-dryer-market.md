<p><p>The retractable clothes dryer market can be categorized into single-line and multi-line types. Single-line dryers consist of a single cord or wire that stretches between two points, providing a compact solution for limited spaces. Conversely, multi-line dryers feature multiple lines or cords suspended together, allowing for more laundry to be hung simultaneously. Each type caters to different needs—single-line is ideal for smaller loads, while multi-line is more suitable for larger families or those with frequent laundry demands.</p></p>
