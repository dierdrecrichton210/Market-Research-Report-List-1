<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The premium car tires market is primarily divided into two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric arranged at angles, providing a flexible structure suited for specific applications, mainly in lighter vehicles. In contrast, radial tires utilize a different construction technique where layers run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better performance, fuel efficiency, and durability. Radial tires dominate the premium segment due to their superior handling and comfort, making them the preferred choice for modern vehicles.</p></p>
